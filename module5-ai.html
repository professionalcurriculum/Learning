Work Smarter, Not Harder

This module empowers you to integrate cutting-edge AI, like Google's Gemini, directly into your daily tasks. Learn to automate processes, generate creative content, and unlock insights from your data, transforming your productivity and efficiency across the entire Workspace suite.

Module Curriculum

Session 1: Introduction to AI in Google Workspace

Understand the fundamentals of AI integration, explore the capabilities of Gemini in Docs, Sheets, and Slides, and learn best practices for prompting to get desired results.

Session 2: Smart Composition & Content Generation

Leverage AI to draft emails, reports, and presentations. Learn techniques for refining tone, summarizing long documents, and brainstorming creative ideas with AI as your collaborative partner.

Session 3: AI-Powered Data Analysis in Sheets

Go beyond formulas with AI. Use natural language to generate insights, create complex formulas, build charts, and identify trends in your datasets without manual calculations.

Session 4: Automating Workflows with AI

Discover how to use AI with Google Apps Script and other tools to automate repetitive tasks, manage projects more efficiently, and build simple, intelligent workflows connecting different Workspace apps.

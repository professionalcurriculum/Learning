Connect, Collaborate, and Report

Master the tools that drive modern teamwork. This module covers everything from hosting effective virtual meetings in Google Meet to building dynamic, shareable reports in Looker Studio, ensuring your team stays aligned and informed.

Module Curriculum

Session 1: Advanced Google Meet & Chat Strategies

Move beyond basic calls. Learn to manage large meetings, use breakout rooms, polling, Q&A features, and integrate Chat for persistent, organized team conversations.

Session 2: Introduction to Looker Studio

Connect to various data sources (Sheets, BigQuery, etc.). Understand the Looker Studio interface, create your first interactive dashboard, and explore different chart types and controls.

Session 3: Designing & Sharing Interactive Reports

Learn dashboard design principles for clarity and impact. Add filters, date range controls, and drill-down functionality. Master sharing and collaboration options for stakeholders.

Session 4: Unified Workflows & Reporting

Integrate your tools for a seamless workflow. Embed Looker Studio reports in Google Sites, present data directly in Slides, and establish routines for automated reporting and data-driven team discussions.
